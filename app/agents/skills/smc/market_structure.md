# Market Structure Analysis

## Key Concepts
- Swing High: A candle high with lower highs on both sides
- Swing Low: A candle low with higher lows on both sides
- Higher High (HH) / Higher Low (HL): Bullish structure
- Lower High (LH) / Lower Low (LL): Bearish structure

## Break of Structure (BOS)
- Bullish BOS: Price breaks above a previous swing high in a bullish trend
- Bearish BOS: Price breaks below a previous swing low in a bearish trend
- BOS confirms trend continuation

## Change of Character (CHoCH)
- Bullish CHoCH: In a bearish trend, price breaks above a swing high
- Bearish CHoCH: In a bullish trend, price breaks below a swing low
- CHoCH signals potential reversal

## Multi-Timeframe
- HTF (H4/D) determines overall bias
- LTF (M15/H1) provides entry timing
- Structure must align across timeframes for high-confidence trades
