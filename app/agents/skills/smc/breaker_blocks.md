# Breaker Blocks

## What is a Breaker Block
Forms when an order block fails — price breaks through the OB and the zone flips.

## Detection
- Bullish breaker: A bearish OB broken to the upside — now acts as support
- Bearish breaker: A bullish OB broken to the downside — now acts as resistance

## Trading Rules
- Wait for price to retest the breaker zone
- Enter on rejection at the breaker level
- Stop loss beyond the breaker block
- Breakers represent trapped traders — strong levels
