# Volume Confirmation

## Confirmation Signals
- Volume spike (>1.5x average) at a key level = institutional activity
- Decreasing volume into a level followed by spike = accumulation then move
- Volume divergence (new highs but declining volume) = weakening trend

## Application
- At OB/FVG zone: volume spike on rejection confirms the level
- During BOS: high volume confirms the break is real
- During CHoCH: volume increase confirms character change
