# Position Sizing

## Rules
- Risk maximum 1-2% of account per trade
- Position size = risk_amount / (entry - stop_loss)
- Minimum R:R ratio of 1:2
- Preferred R:R ratio of 1:3 or better

## Stop Loss Placement
- Below/above the order block or FVG zone
- Add 1 ATR buffer beyond the zone
- Never place stop at obvious liquidity level
