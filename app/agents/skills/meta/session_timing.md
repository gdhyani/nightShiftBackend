# Session Timing

## Sessions (UTC)
- Asia: 00:00 - 07:00
- London: 07:00 - 15:00
- New York: 15:00 - 21:00

## Killzones
- London Open: 07:00 - 10:00 UTC
- New York Open: 13:00 - 16:00 UTC
- London Close: 15:00 - 16:00 UTC

## Rules
- Most volume in London and NY sessions
- Best setups form during killzone hours
- Avoid trading 30 min before/after major news
