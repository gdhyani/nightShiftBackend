# Multi-Timeframe Trend Analysis

## Process
1. Start from Daily — determine overall bias
2. Move to H4 — confirm or note divergence
3. Move to H1 — look for entry setups aligned with HTF bias

## Trend Determination
- Series of HH and HL = bullish trend
- Series of LH and LL = bearish trend
- Mixed = ranging/consolidating

## Alignment Rules
- All timeframes aligned = high confidence
- HTF bullish but LTF bearish = possible pullback, wait for realignment
- HTF neutral = reduce size or skip
- NEVER trade against HTF bias
